
# Patient System — Mobile-First Healthcare App

## Overview
A mobile-first patient healthcare app with a blue medical theme, featuring 4 main sections accessible via bottom navigation: Home, Services, Doctors, and Sign In.

## Pages & Features

### 1. Home Page
- Hero section with tagline: "Expert Care & Support" and subtitle about compassionate, personalized medical care
- Quick-access cards linking to Services and Doctors
- "Offered Services" preview section with icons
- Clean blue medical color palette

### 2. Services Page
- List/grid of general medicine services (e.g., General Consultation, Health Check-ups, Vaccinations, Chronic Disease Management, Preventive Care)
- Each service card with an icon, title, and short description
- Clean card-based layout

### 3. Doctors Page
- Doctor profile cards in a list layout showing photo (placeholder avatars), name, specialty, and star rating
- Tap into a doctor detail view showing bio, qualifications, patient reviews, and a placeholder "Book Appointment" button
- Sample reviews with reviewer name, rating, and comment

### 4. Sign In Page (Static)
- Sign in form with email and password fields
- "Create Account" link/toggle to a sign-up form view
- Static only (no real auth) — ready to connect to Supabase later

### 5. Navigation
- Bottom tab navigation bar (Home, Services, Doctors, Sign In) with icons
- Active tab indicator
- Mobile app-like feel

## Design
- **Mobile-first** layout (max-width container, centered on desktop)
- **Blue medical theme** — primary blue (#2563EB-ish), light blue accents, white backgrounds
- Rounded cards, soft shadows, clean typography
- Medical-themed icons (Lucide icons)
